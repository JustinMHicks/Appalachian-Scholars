Appalachia holds the next generation of creative thinkers and innovators.

 
 

Universities and companies realize that a diverse range of experiences in their members are necessary for creating innovative solutions for the problems we face daily. However, these institutions are only equipped to identify talent in a select group of the population: mainly middle and upper class urban and sub-urban dwellers. Many of the residents of Appalachia do not fit this demographic and are excluded from participating in some of the most valuable opportunities to create change. We bridge this gap by using our expertise in identifying and preparing the most intellectually gifted and stimulating young men and women in Appalachia for success in the nations most influential institutions.
